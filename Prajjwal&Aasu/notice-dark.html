Frontend Web Development Competition

Announcing the "Frontend Web Development Competition" for First Semester students! We are organizing the Frontend Web Development Competition from United Tech Club, for the first semester students of B.E. (Computer) and B.E. (Electrical and Electronics) of United Technical College. The theme for this competition is to build a frontend website prototype (with HTML, CSS and JavaScript) for our college, United Technical College. You can obtain the required information to include in your project from the existing college website at https://www.unitedtechnicalcollege.edu.np/. You can take part in the competition either alone or in a group of two people, and no more than that. The competition will last for a month, until the end of July, and the participants will have to submit their final frontend project by July 30, 2021. The submission guidelines will be provided when we are close to the deadline. If you have any queries regarding this competition, you can feel free to let us know through private message on our Facebook page or contact Rupesh Aryal or Binit Ghimire.
